.. / Spidering

Spidering (also known as "crawling") is the process of systematically navigating through a website or network to discover its resources, links, and content structure. This method involves following links and recording the accessible pages, directories, and files. Spidering is similar to how search engines like Google index websites, but in cybersecurity, spidering is often used to gather information about a target for security assessments or penetration testing.
